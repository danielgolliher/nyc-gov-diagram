Each clickable node opens an info panel with a description, key numbers, and a link to the authoritative source (typically NYC's Greenbook).

The three silos correspond to the three modes of NYC governance — Doing (Charter, Mayor, Council, agencies), Reviewing (BPs and CBs), and Intergovernmental (DC and Albany).
